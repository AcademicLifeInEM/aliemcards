Preparation

Assemble appropriate staffing and equipment (SOAPME):

  • Suction
  • Oxygen,
  • Airway adjuncts
  • Personnel
  • Medications
  • Equipment

Select sedative AND analgesic if both are required. Match duration of procedure to duration of drug.
